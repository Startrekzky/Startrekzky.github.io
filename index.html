Dreamworld Education Project

Duties

- Streamline the workflow for online enquiry and booking for Dreamworld's education programs.

- Propose digital solutions to improve the entire user experience.

- Redesign the user interface, conduct user test and review meeting to evaluate the design approaches.

- Implement the front-end pages based on the design.

Context

The existing online booking and enquiry process on Dreamworld website is poorly designed (check the existing booking page). The workflow is unclear and confusing, which delivers bad user experience for our target Audience. It's difficult for them to find the key information or make a booking. Therefore, many of them turn to the customer service, which adds additional workload for our education department.
